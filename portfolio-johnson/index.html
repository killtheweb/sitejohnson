ABOUT

A Latin American designer forging the future from cultural context & disruptive tools.
Based in Dublin 🇮🇪

Lucas DeMiranda is a multidisciplinary designer interested in disruptive tools, deep narrative thoughts on branding, art direction, visual identities, and motion projects. Originally from São Paulo and now based in Dublin, demiranda.cc represents an independent designer seeking future-proof projects.

Just a wee fella who likes to think, talk, search, and make the act of design.

WHO I AM
-
Being half Brazilian and half Portuguese, I was raised in São Paulo in a multicultural environment surrounded by art. With my father being a musician and my mother working as a professor, I had early exposure to art and expression. Since my sweet sixteen, I've been exploring the long and winding road of design.

WHAT I BELIEVE
-
I strongly believe in the power of experimentation and analyzing the cultural context around the design process. As a Latin American guy, I'm looking to express and expand my brazilian way of design. I want to show you that you should have studied more about us when you were at school.

WHAT I DO
-
All about creating inclusive and eye-catching designs using graphic, interactive, gestual or motion tools. My passion lies in branding, packaging, book design, art direction, digital interfaces, motion graphics, creative coding, and 3D.

WHAT I DID
-
With 7 years of hands-on experience in the design world, I've had the privilege of working with diverse big Latin American companies, serving a wide range of clients. My roles have spanned Graphic/Brand Designer, UI Designer, Visual Designer, and Motion Designer.
